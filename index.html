What's PlanetHub?

Welcome to PlanetHub, where we embark on an exciting journey through the cosmos! As a dedicated team participating in NASA's Space Apps Challenge, we are passionate about unraveling the mysteries of exoplanets. Our mission is to create an engaging platform that showcases the incredible diversity of planets beyond our solar system. Through collaboration and creativity, we aim to inspire curiosity and ignite a sense of wonder about the universe we inhabit. Together, we'll delve into the unknown and expand our understanding of the vast cosmos!
